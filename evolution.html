THE THIRD GENERATION.

BMW E36

The BMW E36 is the third generation of the BMW 3 Series, produced from 1990 to 2000. This model featured improved body rigidity and excellent handling, becoming one of the most popular cars of its time. The E36 was offered in various body styles: sedan, coupe, station wagon, convertible and hatchback. The car was available with various engines, including 4- and 6-cylinder engines. The powerful M3 version of the E36 became an icon of sports car production. The model was characterized by safety innovations such as ABS and stabilization system. The BMW E36 gained great popularity due to its excellent dynamics, comfortable driving and stylish design.

THE FOURTH GENERATION.

BMW E46

The BMW E46 is the fourth generation of the BMW 3 Series, produced from 1997 to 2006. This model was a logical continuation of the E36, offering improved technology, design and comfort. The E46 was available in sedan, coupe, wagon, convertible and hatchback body styles.The car was equipped with both 4- and 6-cylinder engines, and the M3 version of the E46 was distinguished by outstanding sports performance. The E46 had improved safety systems, including a stabilization system and new suspension technologies for better handling. The BMW E46 was recognized for its combination of dynamics, comfort and elegance, becoming one of the most successful and popular cars of its time.
