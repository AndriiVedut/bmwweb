THE SEVENTH GENERATION

BMW 3 Series (G20/G21)

The seventh generation of the BMW 3 Series (G20/G21), presented in 2018, became the embodiment of the model’s evolution, preserving its sporty spirit and introducing cutting-edge technologies. The updated design with a larger kidney grille, improved aerodynamics and a modern, minimalist interior emphasizes the vehicle’s dynamism and premium style. The iDrive 7 system and the Live Cockpit Professional digital instrument cluster make operation more convenient and intuitive. The model is equipped with intelligent driver assistance systems such as adaptive cruise control and traffic jam assistant. A wide range of engines, including 4-cylinder and powerful 6-cylinder units, ensures an optimal balance of power and efficiency, while an advanced suspension guarantees the perfect combination of comfort and sporty handling. The BMW 3 Series G20/G21 continues to set standards in the sports sedan segment.
