THE FIFTH GENERATION

BMW 3 Series (E90/E91/E92/E93)

The fifth generation of the BMW 3 Series was produced from 2005 to 2013 and included four body styles: a sedan (E90), a station wagon (E91), a coupe (E92) and a convertible with a hard folding roof (E93). This car became the embodiment of style, technology and sportiness.The model received powerful and economical engines: from 4-cylinder petrol and diesel engines to in-line 6-cylinder and the M3 version with a V8 with a capacity of 420 hp. The xDrive all-wheel drive system appeared for the first time, and the interior was equipped with advanced technologies, including the iDrive system.The E90 combined comfort, dynamics and control, becoming one of the most successful 3 Series models in the history of BMW.

THE SIXTH GENERATION

BMW 3 Series (F30/F31/F34)

The sixth generation of the BMW 3 Series was produced from 2012 to 2019, becoming more advanced and versatile than ever. The lineup included the sedan (F30), wagon (F31), and the unique Gran Turismo liftback (F34).This model featured a refreshed design with more aggressive headlights, a modernized interior, and upgraded materials. The engine range offered efficient 3- and 4-cylinder options, powerful 6-cylinder variants, and hybrid models.A strong focus on technology was evident, with the iDrive system, adaptive suspension, safety features, and connected services taking the 3 Series to new heights. The BMW F30 maintained the perfect balance of comfort and sportiness, solidifying its reputation as a benchmark in its class.
